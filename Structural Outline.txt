Zombie Survival RTS:
1.There will be 2 main entities:
	1.Units
		1.Zombies are the main adversary in the game. When alerted, they will work tirelessly to wipe your colony from existence
		2.Soldiers protect your colony and its workers from zombies. Soldiers can be in infantry regiments of can be assigned to fixed fortifications such as on walls
		3.Workers build buildings and make the utility buildings function
	2.Buildings
		1.Farm: gives food
		2.Mine: gives metal
		3.Munitions Factory: turns metal into Munitions
		4.power plant: gives power to all other buildings
		5.training grounds
		6.walls
